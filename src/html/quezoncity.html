A LEGACY OF EXCELLENCE - DHVSU

Founded in 1861, DHVSU is recognized as the oldest vocational school in Far East Asia. Originally established as Escuela de Artes y Oficios de Bacolor, the institution has evolved over the years, shaping generations of skilled professionals. It has undergone multiple transformations, including its renaming to Don Honorio Ventura College of Arts and Trades (DHVCAT) in 1978 and later becoming Don Honorio Ventura Technological State University (DHVTSU) in 2009
